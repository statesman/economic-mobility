BUILDING A DIVIDE

Headline for wealth story

Years of housing discrimination helped limit African-Americans’ ability to own property and create wealth in Austin. The impact of those policies is still being felt today.

A SEPARATED CITY

Headline for divide story

As Austin grows, it’s becoming more economically divided. The city’s African-American population is actually declining, and minorities face higher poverty rates and more-isolated neighborhoods than their white counterparts. If that doesn’t change, it could cost billions to the area’s economy - and allow poverty to continue its generational spread.
